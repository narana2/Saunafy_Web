Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an Apple Watch to use Saunafy?

Not at all. While an Apple Watch enhances the experience with convenient in-session tracking, the iPhone app provides complete functionality. The Watch is particularly helpful for monitoring sessions in hot environments where using your phone isn't practical.

What does Saunafy cost?

Saunafy offers a free tier that includes basic session tracking and recovery insights. Our Premium subscription at $4.99/month unlocks advanced AI-powered insights, unlimited session history, and personalized recommendations.

How do you handle my health data and privacy?

Your privacy is our priority. All health data remains securely within Apple's ecosystem. We only access the information you choose to share and use it solely to provide personalized insights. We never sell or share your data with third parties.

I'm new to contrast therapy - is this app suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. Saunafy is designed to help users at every level understand their body's response to hot and cold therapy. You'll learn optimal session durations, recovery patterns, and what works best for your individual physiology.

Does Saunafy integrate with other fitness platforms?

Yes. Through Apple Health integration, your Saunafy sessions automatically sync with other fitness apps that access HealthKit data. Your contrast therapy sessions also contribute to your daily activity goals and close your Apple Watch rings.

What types of facilities and setups does Saunafy support?

Saunafy works with any hot and cold therapy setup - from professional spa facilities to home saunas, gym steam rooms, cold showers, natural bodies of water, or ice baths. The focus is on tracking your therapy sessions, regardless of the equipment.
